Help & Support

How to Report a Complaint? ⌄

Fill the complaint form on the homepage, upload issue image, detect location and submit your complaint.

How Complaint Status Works? ⌄

Every complaint starts with Pending. Admin can later change it to In Progress or Resolved.

What Issues Can I Report? ⌄

You can report garbage issues, road damage, drainage problems, water leakage, traffic issues and more.
